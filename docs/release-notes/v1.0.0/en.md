## What's new in 1.0.0

- The accessibility permission step during first-time setup now works smoothly: Tutti adds itself to the system list automatically and switches to "granted" the moment you turn it on — no more adding it by hand or being stuck on "not authorized."
- The permission prompt no longer pops up before the welcome screen has had a chance to explain why it's needed.
- The "Credits" link in Settings now opens the sponsor page.

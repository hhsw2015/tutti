## What's new in 0.2.4

- The settings panel no longer turns gray when you switch the theme to "Match System."
- If the app quits unexpectedly, your original speaker is restored automatically the next time you open it.
- The update prompt now appears on the screen you're actually using, instead of always on the main display.
- Added a "Contact support" link and a clear refund note in Settings.
- Simplified the wording in Settings so it's easier to follow.
